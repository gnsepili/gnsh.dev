## Why Write Daily?

Writing is thinking made visible. As an AI engineer, I spend most of my time reading papers, experimenting with models, and debugging pipelines. But rarely do I stop to articulate *why* a particular approach works, or what I actually learned from a failed experiment.

This blog is my attempt to change that.

## What to Expect

I'll be writing about:

- **AI & NLP research** — breaking down papers, sharing intuitions, and documenting experiments
- **LLMs in practice** — lessons from building RAG systems, fine-tuning models, and deploying at scale
- **Engineering craft** — the unglamorous but essential work of making AI systems reliable
- **Learning in public** — honest reflections on what I'm figuring out as I go

## The Rules

1. Write every day, even if it's short
2. Prioritize clarity over cleverness
3. Share what I actually learned, not what sounds impressive

Let's see where this goes.
